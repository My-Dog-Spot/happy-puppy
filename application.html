Let's get your application for a new Puppy Started!

Requirements for your new puppy

Before you bring your puppy home you need be prepared for the following :

I have the space for the puppy I am considering and can provide a dedicated area solely for the new puppy

I have researched my new puppy and can provide proper care, veterinary attention and proper nutrition

This puppy is not a gift for another. I am a mature person and will provide the majority of care for my new puppy

I pledge to spay or neuter my puppy

I will follow up with proper registration, maintain an ID tag and will microchip my new puppy.
